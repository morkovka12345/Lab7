Book catalogue

We have selected for you the best books of their time, which to this day have millions of fans around the world!

«1984», G. Orwell

Rated: 

Every day Winston Smith rewrites history along the new line of the Ministry of Truth. With every lie he puts on paper, Winston grows more and more detested by the Party, which is not interested in anything but power, and which does not tolerate dissent. But the more Winston tries to think differently, the more difficult it becomes for him to avoid arrest, because Big Brother is always watching you...

«Little men», L. May

Rated: 

The book describes a private school for boys. Quite free rules of behavior reign in it, however, the pupils - so different in character and outlook on life - imperceptibly become real men. The difficulties and mistakes that boys first make and then correct themselves only help them learn to be brave and honest, to appreciate friendship and devotion.

«Lord of the Flies», W. Golding

Rated: 

A strange, scary, and endlessly compelling book. The story of well-bred boys who suddenly find themselves on a desert island. A philosophical parable about what can happen to people who have forgotten about love and mercy. A grotesque anti-utopia, a warning novel and, of course, a reminder of the fragility of the world in which we all live.
